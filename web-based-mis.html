Web-based MIS

Turning distributed project activity into usable management information.

A clearer explanation of the historical monitoring, evaluation, and knowledge-management concept.

The challenge

Manual tracking could not scale across dispersed field activities.

Project work involved consultants, NGOs, service providers, farmer groups, cooperatives, agribusinesses, committees, and associations operating across multiple locations.

Capture activity, financial, output, and indicator data from field implementation.

03

04

Learn

Preserve project documents and knowledge for stakeholders and future work.

System scope

Planning and evaluation

Monitoring and Evaluation MIS

Document and resource library
