From the original site

Agriculture was presented as both production and enterprise.

The old repository included field photographs covering farming, team activity, planting, and beekeeping. They are retained here as part of the project record.

Implementation depended on people and partnerships.

The project model involved farmer groups, cooperatives, service providers, local organisations, and project management teams.

High-value agriculture included varied rural enterprises.

Support extended beyond crop production to technology, post-harvest systems, and market-oriented activities.
