Design strategy

A public-service interface should earn trust before asking for attention.

01

Content first

Readable typography, clear dates, and predictable page structures replace ornamental UI.

02

Progressive enhancement

Every page works without JavaScript; interaction adds convenience rather than dependence.

03

Honest states

Archived links and nonfunctional services are labelled instead of impersonating live systems.
